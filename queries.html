Q. How do you do online bus reservation on Seat Smart?

Ans: Booking a bus ticket online in India is easy with Seat Smart. Simply enter the Leaving from -- Going to destination details along with the date you wish to travel in the bus search option on the site. Within seconds you will be given a list of buses availability for your route. Select your bus that suits you best for you. Then just follow the simple steps to the ticket booking payment process and your seat will be reserved for your bus journey.

Q. Why is flight ticket booking the cheapest on Seat Smart?

Ans: Seat Smart directly searches multiple airline websites for the cheapest fares. Many airlines prefer to sell their cheapest flight tickets on ixigo. ixigo also searches through millions of routes to find the cheapest possible route for your flight journey.

Q. How can I cancel flight ticket booking?

Ans: To cancel your air ticket booking, follow the given steps:
  • Visit Seat Smart homepage or open the Seat Smart app.
  • Login using the registered mobile no. or email address.
  • Click on your name icon on the top right corner of the screen, a drop-down menu will open.
  • Select ‘My Trips’ and your booked ticket history will appear.
  • Click on the flight ticket you wish to cancel.
  • Select “Proceed to cancellation” and cancel your ticket.

Q. How do I check my seat number on a train?

Ans:To check information about your seat on the train, check your PNR status. This includes the booking status that shows whether you train ticket is confirmed, or it is on the waiting list (WL) or is it under reservation against cancellation (RAC).

Q. How can my train ticket be confirmed?

Ans: About four hours before departure, waitlists are cleared continuously. If the train’s quotas are not filled, the empty seats are released for general public when the chart is prepared. Last minute cancellation will also help you get a confirmed train seat.
